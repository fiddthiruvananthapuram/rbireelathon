About the Campaign

Kerala and Lakshadweep's fight against
Illegitimate loan apps. Mule accounts. Digital fraud.

The Reserve Bank of India, Thiruvananthapuram believes the most powerful voices in this fight are yours. Reelathon 2026 invites undergraduate students across Kerala and Lakshadweep to create original Malayalam reels that educate, warn, and inspire communities about financial safety.

The top entries will be screened at the closing ceremony — a gala event where winners will be decided live. Winning reels may also be used by RBI for financial literacy awareness and outreach, with due credit to the creators.

What to Create

Choose Your Theme

Your reel must be based on one of the three themes below. Entries not aligned to a theme may not be evaluated.

A

Illegitimate Loan Apps

Expose the dangers of unregulated digital lending — how they trap borrowers, misuse personal data, and destroy livelihoods.

B

Mule Accounts

Shed light on how ordinary bank accounts are weaponised in fraud chains — and the devastating consequences for unsuspecting victims.

C

Cyber Hygiene in Digital Banking

Educate viewers on safe digital banking habits — password security, phishing awareness, and protecting personal financial data online.

Before You Enter

Rules & Guidelines

  • 01Open only to currently enrolled UG college students of Kerala and Lakshadweep.
  • 02Team members must be from the same college.
  • 03Entries must be submitted in the names of two members. Valid college ID cards for both will be required.
  • 04One submission per team only. Multiple registrations by the same participants will result in disqualification.
  • 05Your reel must be an original creation made specifically for this competition — not previously submitted or published anywhere else.
  • 06Content must be based on one of the three listed themes. Entries not aligned to a theme may not be evaluated.
  • 07AI tools may be used for editing, graphics, subtitles, or voiceovers — but the concept, scripting, and performance must be original. Fully AI-generated entries will be disqualified.
  • 08Avoid misleading, offensive, defamatory, political, or promotional content. RBI's name or logo, if used, must be represented respectfully and appropriately.
  • 09Entries will be judged on: Relevance to Theme · Creativity & Presentation · Clarity & Brevity · Overall Production Quality.
  • 10Submit your reel along with college ID cards of both credited members and a signed declaration of originality and no copyright infringement.
  • 11Any attempt to influence the judging process will result in disqualification.
  • 12RBI reserves the right to use submitted reels for financial literacy awareness and outreach, with due credit to creators.
  • 13The decision of the judges shall be final and binding. No correspondence will be entertained.
